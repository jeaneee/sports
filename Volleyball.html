What is Volleyball?

Volleyball is a team sport in which two teams of six players are separated by a net. Under regulated regulations, each team aims to earn points by grounding a ball on the opposing teams court. Since tokyo 1964, it has been a part of the official program of the summer olympic games.

Understanding the rules volleyball

Maximum of three hits per side

Team will rotate each time they win the serve

Player can not hit a ball twice (a block is not considered)

6 players on a team, 3 on the front row and 3 on the back row

The first team to reach 25 points and by at least two points wins a set

Server must be behind the end line (dark green/blue) when serving the ball
