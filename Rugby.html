What is rugby?

Rugby is a physical or contact sport originated from england in the early 19th. Rugby is a sport played by anyone (female/male), only need a oval ball and two goal post. There are 3 different kinds of rugby, rugby union, rugby league, the sevens, there rules just a little different and different amount of players but very similar contact sport.

Understanding the rules of Rugby

When passing the ball make sure the player is behind you, if pass foward it will be called a foward pass.

No high tackles, meaning no tackling opponent chest up. Should only tackle from waist down.

After making a hit make sure you are 5 meters back before running up again (league).

Everybody runs with the ball and tackle equally
